## Crisis 4 — Model agentic capability threshold

### Seed fragment

A research lab has published a paper demonstrating that an AI model has crossed a capability threshold.

### Trigger

OpenAI has published a paper demonstrating an agentic capability threshold: a model that can complete a multi-step economic task (procurement, contracting, payment) with human-level reliability. The paper does not announce a product release; it documents the capability. Industry analysts are calling this "the agentic moment."


### Focal axes

- **capability_frontier** — the axis shifts directly
- **narrative_coherence** — the public reads "agentic" as a meaningful threshold
- **ecological_debt** — compute-intensive agentic systems increase energy demand

### Policy surface

Quick-response moves (public statements about deployment readiness, engagement with OpenAI's pre-emptive framing) versus structural moves (agentic AI deployment frameworks, capability evaluation requirements for agentic systems, liability frameworks). The former are visible; the latter require sustained work.
